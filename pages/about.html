About

My Story

I always loved breathtaking designs and aesthetics, so graphic design seemed like the job for me... but there was one problem. I wanted to solve real problems and create meaningful designs. That is when I stumbled upon UX design. a

UX design taught me how to build an engaging product that meets people’s needs in a life-changing way. I started to master new skills like research, spotting issues and opportunities, analyzing information, and turning research insights into ideas.

I completed several projects at CareerFoundry under the mentorship of experienced UX designers, where I showed my ability to utilize research, communicate, and prototype a final solution. I find UX is about spotting problems and finding innovative solutions. I am excited to use my UX skills to help businesses identify user pain points and find groundbreaking solutions.
